Our Story

About LiveAtlas

A platform built not for profit — but for people. Every decision we make comes back to one question: does this make the world more accessible?

Why We Exist

Travel Changes People

When you walk through a market in Jaipur, stand at the edge of a Scandinavian fjord, or feel the silence of Antarctica — something inside you shifts. Assumptions dissolve. Empathy grows. You understand, maybe for the first time, that humanity is one family.

But most people will never get on a plane. Not because they don't want to — because they can't. Financial barriers. Physical limitations. Time. Responsibilities.

LiveAtlas was born to fix that. To make the transformative power of travel available to every soul on Earth — and to build livelihoods for local youth in the process.
