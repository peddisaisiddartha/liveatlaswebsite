Our Story

LiveAtlas was born from a simple idea: what if everyone could explore the world without leaving their home? Founded in 2023, we've pioneered the fusion of VR technology with live human guides to create authentic, immersive travel experiences.

Today, we're proud to connect thousands of travelers with local guides worldwide, offering unprecedented access to destinations while supporting local economies and promoting sustainable tourism practices.
